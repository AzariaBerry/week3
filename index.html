The Amazing Life of Polar Bears

Polar bears are fasinatings animals. They are located in Anartica, and spend most of their lives on the sea ice of the artic ocean. They have a thick layer of body fat and thick fur that repels water and the cold. They must eat seals to sustain their amount of body fat.

  • status: Vulnerable
  • Population: 22,000-31,000
  • Scientific Name: Ursus maritimus
  • Weight: 800-1,300 Lbs
  • Length: 6-9 feet
